    Abdul Miyaa

    Abdul is the proprietor of a soda shop 'All In One General Store' situated near the society entrance. Male members gather at Abdul's shop for interaction and enjoy soda. He is a close friend to the members of Gokuldham Society. He is best known for his soda. He also sells toys, sports equipment and general store items, so that neither kids nor ladies have to go out to buy them. Abdul also offers the service of home delivery. He is disciplined and always ready to help others. He assists in making arrangements, decoration for a programme to be held in the society along with Tapusena and arranges for all type of workers and labors, whenever required. He wholeheartedly participates in every religious festival and celebrations despite being a Muslim. He is not a resident of Gokuldham Society but treats the society as if his own home.
