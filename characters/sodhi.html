    Sardar Roshan Singh Harjeet Singh Sodhi

    Roshan Singh Sodhi is very enthusiastic, optimistic and energetic character in the show. However, he is short-tempered and lashes out at anyone trying to mess with residents of Gokuldham Society. He owns a garage called 'Sodhi Garage'. He is a devout Sikh and always ready to help others. Sodhi is a man with a golden heart and he freely expresses romantic feelings towards his wife, Roshan Kaur Sodhi.
